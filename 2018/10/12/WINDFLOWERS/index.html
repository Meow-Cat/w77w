WindFlowers(风飞花)

​Wind Flowers，Wind Flowers

my father told me not to go near them

he said he feared them always and he told me that they carried him away

Wind Flowers,beautiful windflowers

I count not wait to touch them

To smell them I held them closely

And now , I connot break away

Their sweet bouquet disappears like the vapor in the desert

So take a warning, son

Wind Flowers,ancient windflowers

their beauty capture every young dreamer

who lingers near them

But ancient windflowers, I love you
